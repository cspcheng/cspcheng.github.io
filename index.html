Short Bio

I am Peng Cheng, a researcher with the title of Zijiang Young Scholar in Department of Data Science and Engineering at East China Normal University from March 2019. I received my BS and master degrees at Xi'an Jiaotong Univeristy in 2012 and 2014. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering, supervised by Prof. Lei Chen, at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2017. Then I worked with Prof. Lei Chen as a Post-Doc from October 2017 to March 2019.

Research Interests

Crowdsourcing, Spatial Crowdsourcing, Ridesharing, Graph Databases
